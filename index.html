About Us

Cadillac Lounge opened it's doors in 1954. Great musicians have entered our stage through the years.
The bar have a wide selection of beverages to accomaodate all tastes.
Keep your instrument in tune and join our very own Cadillac Lounge bluesband for our weekly Wednesday jam sessions! Visit our Event page for more information and bookings.

Live music sessions Friday to Sunday evening with established musicians from all over the world! We're a proud sponsor of the annual Chicago Blues Fest and Chicago Jazz fest.

Fatboy are definitely the blues kings of the USA. "Sing Me the News" is the title of jazz singer/songwriter Janet Doeson's new album! A loosely held concept album about the different stages of a crisis and the desire to move on out of it. Musically, as always with Janet, it is a seamless genre mix, held together by the red thread that is her voice, tone dialing and instrumentation. Her art pop contains elements of jazz, blues, country and orchestral landscapes.
