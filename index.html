About Us

Cadillac Lounge opened it's doors in 1954. Great musicians have entered our stage through the years. The bar have a wide selection of beverages to accomaodate all tastes. Keep your instrument in tune and join our very own Cadillac Lounge bluesband for our weekly Wednesday jam sessions! Visit our Event page for more information and bookings.

Live music sessions Friday to Sunday evening with established musicians from all over the world! We're a proud sponsor of the annual Chicago Blues Fest and Chicago Jazz fest.
